How it works?

Enter your academic performance from past semester exams, and our ML model will conduct a comprehensive analysis. It considers various factors asked during evaluation, such as IQ, free time, study hours, backlogs, and more.

Recognizing where to invest time is pivotal for effective planning. This awareness fosters an understanding of the importance of strategic time management, enhancing academic performance. Pinpointing specific areas for improvement tailors study sessions, optimizing efforts and leading to improved comprehension and higher scores.

Students receive personalized guidance and access to top-tier resources on content-related media platforms like YouTube, aiding them in studying more effectively for areas of weakness. This provides valuable insights, ultimately improving academic performance.
